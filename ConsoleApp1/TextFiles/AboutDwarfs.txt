I don't think dwarves need much introduction because they're pretty much what you'd expect them to be in any fantasy setting.
They're boisterous, greedy, deeply protective of their traditions, and always eager to chop a goblin or orc in two. 

Ability bonus: +1 wisdom.

Racial features:

Dwarven Weapon Training: Proficient with axes and hammers.
Dwarven Resilience: Advantage on saving throws against poison and resistance to poison damage.
