Like dwarves, elves in this world pretty much live up to all the stereotypes.
Living for thousands of years, elves have an acute interest and understanding of magic in all its forms and adore artistic pursuits, nature, and being mildly condescending. 

Ability bonus: +2 dexterity, +1 intelligence

Racial features:

Keen Senses: Proficient with the Intuition skill.
Fey Ancestry: Advantage on saving throws against being charmed, magic can't put you to sleep.
Elven Weapon Training: Proficient with swords and bows.
Condescending aura: Disadvantage with the persuasion skill.
Darkvision: You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light and in darkness as if it were dim light, but you can't see color.
