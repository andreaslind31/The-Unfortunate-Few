Unless you're reading this article from an intergalactic spaceship, you're probably pretty familiar with the human race.
They're the most common race in the land of Faerûn and are known for being clever, tenacious, and adaptable.
They're also a jack of all trades because instead of favoring certain ability scores or having special racial perks, humans simply get a small bonus to all of their ability scores.

Ability bonus: +1 to strength, dexterity, constitution, wisdom, intelligence, charisma.
